About Global Path Immigration

Global Path Immigration is a leading Canadian immigration consultancy dedicated to helping individuals and families achieve their immigration goals. Founded on principles of integrity, transparency, and personalized service, we understand that every client's journey is unique.

Our team of experienced and regulated immigration consultants (RCICs) provides expert advice and comprehensive support throughout the entire immigration process, from initial assessment to final settlement. We are committed to making your transition to Canada as smooth and successful as possible.
